Ava-Nicole Fincher

Ava-Nicole Fincher is an 18-year-old professional dancer with a passion for movement that has defined her life from a young age. Dance has always been more than just a hobby for Ava-Nicole; it is her true calling and a central part of her identity. From ballet to contemporary, hip-hop to jazz, she has dedicated countless hours to mastering a wide range of dance styles. Her commitment to perfecting her craft has earned her recognition as a skilled and versatile dancer in her local community. As Ava-Nicole embarks on the next chapter of her journey, she is preparing to take a significant step in her career: attending college to pursue a professional degree in dance. With plans to further her education and continue developing as a dancer, she is excited to immerse herself in a collegiate environment where she can refine her technique, learn from experts, and explore new creative avenues. Her ultimate goal is to make a lasting impact in the dance world, whether through performance, choreography, or teaching.
